### 1. **Prerequisite Check**
- Verifies courses don't have too many prerequisites
- Ensures recommendations are appropriate for first-years
- Flags if >2 courses require prerequisites

- Encourages exploration

- Checks for versatile recommendations

### 5. **Course Level**
- Validates course difficulty is appropriate
- Flags upper-level (300+) courses for first-years
- Ensures reasonable challenge level
